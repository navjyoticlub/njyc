About NJYC Nepal

SOCIAL ECO INNOVATIVE TRUST, familiar to people as NJYC Nepal in brief. NJYC is an entirely non-profit making, non-governmental and social development organization working in rural and need-based community with the prime objective of uplifting socio-economic and management of natural resources. This organization has been serving to the people of community to enhance livelihood and resource productivity. We are trying our best efforts with the initiation of local people for introducing different programme. At present this organization has been working in the field of education to improve the Quality of education of government school. The main aim to establish NJYC is to balance the society and environment through organizing different programs in field of education, agriculture, health, women and youth empowerment through overall focusing on environment. It is working for social development, natural resource management, income generating activities, gender and social inclusion, Womenâ€™s Empowerment, Infrastructure, micro enterprises development, Capacity Building and awareness, etc since fiscal year 2011/012.
